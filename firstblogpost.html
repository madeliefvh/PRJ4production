Embracing Style on and off the Pitch

As a professional soccer player, my life revolves around the game I love, the relentless training, and the unyielding pursuit of excellence on the field. However, there's another aspect of my life that has recently blossomed into something truly exciting - my partnership with Lacoste, facilitated by the amazing team at Kick Her Goals. Kick Her Goals, a platform dedicated to empowering female athletes, approached me with an opportunity that not only resonated with my passion for soccer but also appealed to my love for style and fashion. They had teamed up with Lacoste to offer me a brand deal that would not only elevate my off-field wardrobe but also empower me to represent a brand synonymous with elegance and sporty sophistication. The deal included a range of perks that immediately caught my attention. Firstly, I was thrilled to receive a selection of Lacoste's iconic apparel and accessories. From classic polo shirts to stylish sneakers, each piece exuded the timeless allure that Lacoste is renowned for. As a female athlete, it's important for me to feel confident and comfortable both on and off the pitch, and Lacoste's designs effortlessly combine functionality with fashion-forward flair. But the benefits didn't stop there. Kick Her Goals went above and beyond to sweeten the deal by offering me a generous 25% discount code that I could share with my fans, friends, and fellow athletes. Not only does this allow me to spread the love for Lacoste within my community, but it also gives everyone the chance to experience the quality and style that Lacoste represents. One of the highlights of this partnership was undoubtedly the exhilarating photoshoot arranged by Kick Her Goals and Lacoste. Stepping in front of the camera in my Lacoste attire was a surreal experience - a stark contrast to the intensity of the soccer field, yet equally empowering in its own right. Capturing the essence of athleticism and elegance simultaneously was a challenge I embraced wholeheartedly, and I couldn't be prouder of the stunning images that resulted from this collaboration. Beyond the tangible benefits of free clothes and discount codes, this partnership symbolizes something much deeper to me. It represents the growing recognition and support for female athletes in the world of fashion and beyond. It's a testament to the fact that women can excel in sports while embracing their individuality and sense of style. It's a reminder that we are more than just athletes - we are multifaceted individuals with passions and interests that extend far beyond the confines of the soccer field. I am immensely grateful to Kick Her Goals and Lacoste for believing in me and for giving me the opportunity to be a part of something truly special. As I continue to kick goals on the field, I'll do so with the confidence and style that Lacoste has instilled in me, both as an athlete and as a woman. And with Kick Her Goals by my side, I know that the possibilities for empowerment and inspiration are endless. -Shakuntala de Jong
