Kick Her Goals

Kick Her Goals is a platform that connects women with the same passion for soccer.

It was founded to bring together these different mindsets to help and support eachother.

Our Vision

We advocate for gender equality in all spheres of life, including sports and professional pursuits. We work towards a future where this career path is sustainable for women to pursue, resulting in a world where women's football is as popular as men's.

Our Mission

Giving women football players the opportunity to connect over the fact that pursuing a career after their football career is difficult, because it is still not as sustainable as it is for men.

Our Values

We believe in empowering women by providing a platform for connection and support, acknowledging the challenges they face in transitioning from football careers to other professional pursuits, especially amidst the responsibilities of motherhood. We advocate for the recognition and support of women athletes beyond their playing days, championing initiatives and policies that facilitate career development opportunities, particularly for those balancing the demands of for example motherhood alongside professional aspirations. We value authenticity and honesty in our interactions, fostering genuine connections and conversations that acknowledge the complexities of women's experiences post-football career, embracing vulnerability, and celebrating the diverse paths taken by individuals in pursuit of their goals, including navigating the intersections of career aspirations.
